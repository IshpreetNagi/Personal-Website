U.V.Y.C Website

Visit Website

Details

The U.V.Y.C (Urban Vision Youth Council), is an organization of motivated teenagers with a passion for urban planning and sustainability who work directly with the city of Brampton to increase youth participation, create more leadership opportunities, and promote a sustainable and community-oriented urban plan for the city. To view the fully functioning website, please refer to the link embedded in the button above.

As a direct result of my experience in online site creation, another individual and I were offered the opportunity by the council to design and develop a fully functioning website for them. I was given complete freedom in terms of design and overall aesthetic as well as the medium used to bring the site to fruition.


Part of the 'About Us' page on the website

Despite being heavily experienced in HTML and Javascript, my partner and I decided to use Wix.com, a browser based online site creator, as our creation method for the site. The reason for choosing an online offered creator rather than creating the website from scratch using such languages as HTML, Javascript, and CSS is that coding the site from nothing would have taken a long period of time, however the council wanted the final product within a rather reasonable time frame. So in order to meet the time restraint, it was decided to use the streamlined creation method offered by Wix for the site.

Since the council works in collaboration with the city of Brampton government, much of the site inspiration behind the site’s aesthetic and outline was directly taken from the website created and operated by the city of Brampton. That resemblance of elements between the U.V.Y.C website and city of Brampton website would help to further solidify the collaboration between the two and help to better showcase their relation to the public.


'Contact' page on the website

In the end, the U.V.Y.C website created by my partner and I, had many interactive and moving components to it without sacrificing simplicity to make the site feel much more developed and appealing to the young audience the council targets. While still being easy to follow for users of all variety of ages and experience with technology. The site informs the reader on all the information regarding the projects and actions conducted by the council but still has an overall theme and aesthetic that is unique and modern while still resembling the official City of Brampton website.
